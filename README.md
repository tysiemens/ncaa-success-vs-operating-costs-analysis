# NCAA Football Operating Cost vs Success Analysis

A data analysis and visualization comparing NCAA football operating success to on-field success from 2017-2022

---

## Overview

This project analyzes and visualizes the correlation between a team's football operating expenses and SRS (team strength), compares across conferences and years, and identifies the most "monetarily-efficient" team from 2017-2022. The goal is to identify which teams spend their money most efficiently to create a good football program, as well as identify key differences in these patterns by year and conference (including Power 5 vs Group of 5). 

---

## Features

- Data cleaning
- Data visualization
- Multiple linear regression
- Feature engineering
  
---

## Dataset

Source: USA Today NCAA Finances: Revenue & Expenses by School and Sports reference

Contains over 600 NCAA D1 football teams by year, including win statistics and operating expenses

Important variables:
- `Year`
- `School`
- `SRS`
- `Football.Total.Operating.Expenses`
- `AwayScore`
